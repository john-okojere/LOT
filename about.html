WE PREACH THE TRUTH OF GOD'S WORD BY THE POWER OF THE HOLY GHOST!

Layers of Truth Church aspires to be more than a guide; we aim to be a lighthouse, navigating away from confusing doctrines and misleading teachings. In the midst of the multitude of voices, we stand firm, preserving the sound of truth in the earth. Our dedication stems from the understanding that eternal life is found in knowing the God that is true, the One who cannot lie. knowing fully well, that our enemy is the deceiver of the whole world. We are the one called out from amongst them, We are sanctified by the truth, we are the Members of the church, The church is the pillar and ground of Truth.

Our Vision

From the least to the greatest, all will know God.

From the towering mountains to the smallest corners, our vision at Layers of Truth Church is as clear as the morning sun: Every soul, regardless of age or background, will encounter the profound knowledge of God.

>Our vision is for everyone, regardless of age or background, to come into the knowledge of God. We aim to foster spiritual maturity, guiding individuals away from being easily swayed by varying doctrines or deceptive influences. Siloam 2024

Siloam is a non-denominational 4-day conference hosted annually by The Layers of Truth Church, Kaduna. It is usually a time of in-depth study of God's word, line upon line and precept upon precept, bringing great revelation light that leads to restoration, renewal and revival.

Disciple's Charge

Disciple's Charge is a weekly program designed to rekindle and reinforce the timeless teachings of Jesus Christ. It serves as a platform for men and women of God to expound on these foundational doctrines, shedding new light on their profound significance. Emphasizing that being a Christian goes beyond a mere label, the program encourages believers to actively continue in the words of Jesus, distinguishing between mere church attendance and the transformative journey of a true disciple.

Baptism

Baptism holds profound significance in our faith journey, as expressed in Romans 6:3-4: 'Know ye not, that so many of us as were baptized into Jesus Christ were baptized into his death? Therefore, we are buried with him by baptism into death: that like as Christ was raised up from the dead by the glory of the Father, even so we also should walk in newness of life.' Through baptism, we symbolically partake in Christ's death and resurrection, embracing a renewed life and a deeper connection with our Savior.
